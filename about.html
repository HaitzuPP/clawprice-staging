About OpenClaw Index

What is OpenClaw Index?

OpenClaw Index tracks the market performance of tokens in the OpenClaw and Moltbook ecosystem. Data sourced from CoinGecko.

We provide live price tracking, market cap rankings, volume data, and percentage changes for the top tokens in the ecosystem. The index auto-refreshes every 60 seconds to keep you up to date.

Data Sources

All market data is fetched directly from the CoinGecko API. Prices, market caps, and volume figures are denominated in USD.
